Technical Communications is a course dedicated to self improvement.
The course focuses on written and oral communication strategies and personal development.
  • Over the duration of this course, students focused on preparing for whichever field they choose to pursue.
  • In order to prepare for our careers, throughout this course we spent time
    • Refining our resumes and cover letters.
    • Attending career fairs.
    • Creating User Instructions.
    • Creating a mock Press Release.
    • Delivering potential solutions to on campus problems.
    • Creating a delivering oral presentations.
  • Alongside the course work, we learned developed essential skills such as
    • Audience Awareness.
    • Visual Communication.
    • Creating a brand.
    • Research Topics & Evaluate the information provided.
    • Collaborative Problem Solving.
    • Utilizing rhetorical strategies such as Ethos(Credibility), Logos(Logic), and Pathos(Emotion) within our writings.
  • And finally, our last assignment was to create a personal website( Yes, the one you're looking at now! ).
